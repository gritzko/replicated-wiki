BRO-003: BRO-002 status-row regressions — sub-relay rows + patch codes

BRO-002 (landed 25abfece+acbda302) converged every verb onto ONE per-(sub)module table hunk rendered by the single HUNKu8sFeedBanner path. That land regressed status-row output in two cases that four previously-green tests assert. Both are rendering/relay gaps, not logic gaps — the verbs do the work (counters and prose lines are correct), but the per-file/per-status ROW no longer surfaces in the report a consumer parses.
